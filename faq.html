FAQ & Policies

Order & Payment

Q: What is your minimum order quantity?

A: We accept small trial orders for most items. minimum order quantity varies by product.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept telegraphic transfer, bank transfer, and other secure payment methods.

Production & Shipping

Q: Are products produced by your company?

A: We do not manufacture products directly. All goods are produced by our qualified partner factories.

Q: How long is production and delivery time?

A: Usually 7–15 working days after payment confirmation, depending on order quantity.

Q: Do you ship to all countries?

A: We ship to most countries around the world. For specific countries and shipping details, please contact us by email.

Quality & Inspection

Q: How do you control product quality?

A: We conduct strict quality inspection before shipment to ensure compliance with standards.

Return & Refund Policy

Q: What if products have quality issues?

A: Please contact us within 7 days after receiving goods with clear photos or evidence. We will check and provide a reasonable solution.

Privacy Policy

We protect your personal information and business data. All information is used only for order processing and service improvement. We will not disclose to third parties without your consent.

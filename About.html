About Timi Trading Limited

Timi Trading Limited is a professional jewelry trading company specializing in high-quality lab-grown diamonds and gemstone jewelry. We are committed to providing reliable wholesale and OEM services for global buyers with excellent quality at competitive prices.

With years of experience in the jewelry industry, we have established stable supply chains and strict quality control systems. Our products include necklaces, rings, earrings, bracelets and custom jewelry, all crafted with precision and attention to detail.

We value long-term cooperation with our customers and strive to provide flexible solutions to meet different market needs. Whether you are a small business or a large retailer, we can tailor our services to your requirements.
